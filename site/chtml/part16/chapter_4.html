CAP

NEMA

National Electrical Manufacturers Association

RECIST

Response Evaluation Criteria In Solid Tumors

SNOMED

Systematized Nomenclature of Medicine

UCUM

Unified Code for Units of Measure

WHO

World Health Organization

EV

DT

DTID
