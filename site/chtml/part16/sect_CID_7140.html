Note

  1. (T-D1403, SRT, "Cranial Cavity") may be used to describe the volume of the entire intra-cranial space (intra-cranial volume or ICV).

  2. (T-A6041, SRT, "Cerebellar Cortex") is the gray matter of the cerebellum (as distinct from (T-A6080, SRT, "Cerebellar white matter")).

  3. (T-A1504, SRT, "Cranial Subarachnoid Space") may be used to describe the volume of the exterior CSF (surrounding the brain, excluding the ventricles).

  4. (T-A1509, SRT, "Cerebellar Subarachnoid Space") may be used to describe the volume of the inferior intracranial CSF space (infra-tentorial).
