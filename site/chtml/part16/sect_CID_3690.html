CID 3690 ECG Control Variables Numeric

This Context Group includes the ECG control variables specified in the ISO/IEEE 11073-10102 nomenclature that take numeric values. The terms are included in the table below for reference; these may not constitute the complete current list (see the ISO/IEEE Standard).
