CID 5002 Organizations

Context Group ID 5002 comprises the object identifier scheme of ISO 8824-1 and ISO 9834-1, when applied to organizational identifiers (see Section 8.2). The Coding Scheme Designator (0008,0102) shall be ISO_OID.
