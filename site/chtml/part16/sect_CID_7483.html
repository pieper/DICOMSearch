Note

  1. T-12771 is used in preference to (T-12770, SRT, "Calcaneus").

  2. T-D0788 is used in preference to carpal joint.

  3. T-11B02 is used in preference to (T-11BF0, SRT, "coccyx") as used for humans, since the animal possess a tail.

  4. T-127EC assumes correspondence between equine hindfoot and human navicular, and T-12450 the equine forefoot navicular and human scaphoid (distal sesamoid).

  5. T-11096 is used for the hock joint.

  6. In a prior version of this table, the code T-D8300 was used for T-15430. Although there is minimal possibility of misinterpretation with SOP Instances that may include the deprecated use, receiving applications should be aware of this change; see Annex J.
