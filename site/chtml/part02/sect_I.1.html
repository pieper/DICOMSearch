I.1 Conformance Statement Overview

This fictional product EXAMPLE-WADO-SERVICE implements the WADO-URI services, the WADO WS services and the WADO RS services for access to DICOM SOP Instances that are stored on an EXAMPLE-PACS-ARCHIVE. The EXAMPLE-WADO-SERVICE is only available as a plug in option for the EXAMPLE-PACS-ARCHIVE. All of the networking, database, and other services are provided by the EXAMPLE-PACS-ARCHIVE. This conformance claim refers to the conformance claim for the EXAMPLE-PACS-ARCHIVE for all such services.
